Overview

Purpose

Intended for users to navigate through different national park locations in the US. They can find parks by state, best seasons, views/features or popularity.

Audience

Outdoorsy users looking to find more information on national parks and tips for traveling them.

Dynamic elements

Home page includes a image carosel of popular parks. The subpage will include a variety of ways to search and filter through the parks.

Wireframes

Home

Two links in the nav bar: home and parks. Home page will have a large hero img with title of the site layered on. This page will feature a carosel with images of the most popular parks to visit. There will be three fun facts/tips at the bottom of the site. The footer will have a short thank you message as well as link to the siteplan and button to the parks page.

Parks

The parks page will have a hero img with little title centered on top. There will be a search bar for users to filter through different parks. There is going to be four filter boxes that users can use to narrow down by state, best season, views/features the park has and popularity among fellow travelers. These filters will have different names that are more creative and direct for a more clear understanding.
